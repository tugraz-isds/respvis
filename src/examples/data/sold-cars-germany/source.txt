Germany Cars Dataset
Dataset scraped from AutoScout24 with information about new and used cars.

AutoScout24 is one of the largest Europe's car market for new and used
cars. We've collected car data from 2011 to 2021.

Edits:

Randomly selected 500 cars from 46,405 cars.
